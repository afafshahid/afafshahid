### 👋 About Me

I'm a Computer Science undergrad (Class of 2027) at **FAST-NUCES Karachi**, building at the intersection of **full-stack development** and **AI/ML**. I like turning research-grade ideas — Bayesian inference, geospatial ML — into software people can actually use.

- 🩺 Built **SehatYaab**, an AI-driven healthcare platform for emergency blood matching, medicine transparency, and symptom-based triage
- 🧠 Built a **Bayesian medical triage system** with a 125-disease knowledge base, using log-space inference for numerical stability
- 🛰️ Trained geospatial ML models on 23 years of satellite data (Google Earth Engine) to predict drought severity across Pakistan croplands
- 👩‍💻 Vice President of **ACM-Women** at FAST-NUCES — organized EmpowerFest, a women-led tech event
- 🌱 Currently sharpening my DSA and shipping more full-stack + ML projects
